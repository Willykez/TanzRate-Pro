TanzRate — Tanzania Forex & Currency Tracker

The most complete Tanzanian Shilling exchange rate app on the Play Store. Track live forex rates, convert between 14+ currencies, monitor official Bank of Tanzania rates, set price alert notifications, and glance at your rates directly from your home screen — all in one beautifully designed app built specifically for TZS users.

──────────────────────────────────────────
  WHAT'S NEW IN VERSION 2.2
──────────────────────────────────────────

🎨  Light & Dark Themes — Switch between a sleek midnight navy dark mode and a crisp white light mode from Settings → Appearance. Every color, card, input, and background adapts instantly.

📱  Home Screen Widget — Add the TanzRate widget to your Android home screen for at-a-glance USD, EUR, GBP, and KES rates without even opening the app. Refreshes automatically every 30 minutes.

🔔  Rich Price Alert Notifications — When a currency crosses your target, a full-featured notification appears in your notification panel — complete with a custom icon, a progress bar showing how close the rate was to your target, and quick-action buttons to open the app or dismiss.

🏦  Official Bank of Tanzania Rates — Live buy/sell rates scraped directly from the Bank of Tanzania website (bot.go.tz) replacing estimated spreads with real, official data.

## TanzRate — Tanzania Forex Tracker

> Track live TZS exchange rates, get notified on price movements, and check official Bank of Tanzania rates — all from a single lightweight Android app.

### What's in this repo

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `TanzaniaForexApp.java` | Main single-Activity app (2 250 lines) |
| `RateWidget.java` | Home screen AppWidgetProvider |
| `AlertNotificationManager.java` | Styled notification builder |
| `rate_widget_layout.xml` | Widget layout (ImageView container) |
| `rate_widget_info.xml` | AppWidgetProviderInfo metadata |

- Standard Android SDK (minSdk 21)
